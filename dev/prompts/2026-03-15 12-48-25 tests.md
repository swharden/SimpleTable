### Bug found and fixed in `StringTable.cs`
`AddColumn(string?)` and `AddColumn(string, IList)` were adding to `ColumnNamesList` but never registering the new column in `ColumnIndexesByName`. This meant any name-based lookup (`IndexOf`, `GetColumnValues(string)`, `table[row, name]`) silently failed for any column added after construction. Both overloads now capture the new index and write it into the dictionary.
